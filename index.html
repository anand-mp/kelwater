FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about waterproofing, our process, materials, and what to expect on-site.

How long does professional waterproofing last?+
Properly installed waterproofing systems last 20–50 years depending on the product type. Crystalline systems are considered permanent. Our workmanship warranty covers the full lifetime of the structure we treat.
Do I need to vacate my home during work?+
In most cases, no. Exterior foundation work and roof waterproofing don't disrupt interior living at all. Interior basement work may require clearing the affected area, but rarely requires the whole family to vacate the property.
What's included in the free inspection?+
A certified technician visits your property, conducts a full moisture audit, identifies all entry points with photo documentation, and provides a written findings report — completely free, no obligation, no sales pressure.
How quickly can work begin after I accept a quote?+
For standard projects, we typically start within 5–10 business days of quote acceptance. Emergency situations are handled within 24 hours. We'll give you a precise confirmed start date on your written quote.
Is the lifetime warranty truly transferable to buyers?+
Yes, completely. Our warranty transfers to any new property owner at zero cost. Simply notify us of the pending sale and we issue updated documentation to the buyer. Many of our clients find it adds measurable value at resale.
Can you assist with insurance claims for water damage?+
Yes. We assist with documentation for insurance claims and work alongside insurance assessors regularly. We provide before/after photo reports, material certificates, waterproofing specs, and full warranty documentation as required.
What causes basement walls to let in water?+
The most common causes are hydrostatic pressure from saturated soil, failed original waterproofing, structural cracks, poor drainage around foundations, and failed penetration seals around pipes and services. We identify and address all of these.
Do you offer a maintenance plan after installation?+
Yes. We offer annual inspection and maintenance plans for both residential and commercial clients. Regular check-ups catch minor issues before they become expensive, and keep your warranty in full standing throughout its term.
